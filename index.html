How to play:

You're bread. Sentient Bread. And you are trying to bake yourself.

Move around using WASD and collect the right ingredients.
But hurry up, you shrink over time!

You can try to slice other loafs with your mouse.
The closer you are to it, the more effective your slice gets,
but watch out, they can cut you as well.
